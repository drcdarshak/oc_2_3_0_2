Important information

How we charge
  • The one-off call-out fee is a single charge that helps cover some of the cost of our movers getting to your origin address and then back from your final address
  • The hourly rate begins when we arrive at your pick-up address and ends when we finish at the destination address
  • Once you have made your $50 deposit you will receive a booking confirmation within 12 hours
  • A minimum of 2 hours applies on all jobs - we charge in 15 minute increments

Payment terms

  • Final payment must be settled on the day of the move in cash or Visa/MasterCard (we do not accept cheques)
  • Should you wish to cancel your booking, your deposit is fully refundable up to 48 hours prior to your scheduled move

Insurance policy

  • Whilst the work of our removalist team is covered under our insurance policy, we recommend you take insurance for your move for things outside our control. You can obtain a quote here

How long will my move take?

  • Every move is different and depends on numerous factors including access, parking and how much furniture you have. A typical 1 bedroom property takes 2-4 hours, 2 bedroom property takes 4-6 hours, 3 bedroom property takes 5-7 hours and a 4 bedroom property 7-10 hours.
